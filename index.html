Capabilities

Research at a different scale

Deep Web Search

Autonomous agents scan the live web, academic repositories, and curated databases to surface the most relevant sources for your topic.

RAG Document Context

Upload your own PDFs and source documents. The retrieval system grounds generation in your private knowledge base alongside live search.

Human-in-the-Loop

Review and approve candidate sources before synthesis. Stay in control of what feeds your final report — no black-box automation.

Critic Loop

A dedicated critic agent reviews drafts for factual accuracy, logical coherence, and coverage gaps — iterating until quality thresholds are met.

Structured Reports

Outputs are fully structured with headings, citations, tables, and source lists — ready for review or immediate publication.

Export Anywhere

Download your report as Markdown or rendered PDF. Share, archive, or feed results back into future research pipelines.

Process

From query to report

01

Define Your Topic

Enter your research question and upload supporting PDF documents for private context. The system understands nuanced, multi-part queries.

02

Deep Search

Agents fan out across the web and your documents, returning ranked candidate sources with summaries, credibility signals, and relevance scores.

03

Human Approval

Review the candidate source list. Approve or reject each source before synthesis begins. Your judgment shapes the final report.

04

Scrape, Write & Critic

Approved sources are fully scraped. A writer agent drafts the report; a critic agent iterates until quality standards are satisfied.

05

Export & Use

Download your structured report in Markdown or PDF. Annotated, cited, and ready for immediate use.
